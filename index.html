Our Mission Statement

Jashan Technology's mission is to be the most sophisticated supplier of custom precision sheet metal manufacturing and related value added services from single piece to high volume production in India. We will attain this customer focused, cost sensitive mission by providing vertically integrated solutions using project management,cutting edge technology and advanced materials planning.

Our Capturing Market Sectors

Petrochemical Industry

Our primary domain, we specialize in tier 2 and tier 3 petrochemical products such as end caps for boilers, clamps, specialized steel covers, domes and much more. Welding, Turning, Milling, Press forming products are ensured to be completed to our customer's specification and time requirement.

Automotive Industry

Products specialized for tankers and trucks with high precision using our in house hydraulic press is always something we will pride upon. Dish end forming, Rings, Manhole covers, mountable steel boxes for valves and rope levers,etc., we have you covered for all your needs on the big rig sector.

Experimental production

Always keeping our minds open for new ideas, in the rare time that we get to ourselves we are always out in the horizon, exploring unknown sectors. With our team of expert manual machinists and welders, we simply refuse to say no to a project. Plastic shredders, Low space transformable furniture, a fully realized student formula car, when we say experimental we mean it. Pitch in your ideas for collabs, we always have our ears open for artistry in any form.
